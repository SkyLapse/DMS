REST-API
===

Every specified URL is prefixed by http://_yoursite.com_/api

### Documents:
- Retreive: /documents/$id (GET)
- Add: /documents/new (POST)
- Edit: /documents/$id (PUT)
- Delete: /documents/$id (DELETE)

### Tags:
- Retreive: /tags/$id (GET)
- Add: /tags/new (POST)
- Edit: /tags/$id (PUT)
- Delete /tags/$id (DELETE)
