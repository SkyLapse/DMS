REST-API
===

Every specified URL is prefixed by http://_yoursite.com_/api/

### Documents:
- Retreive: documents/$objectid (GET)
- Add: documents/new (POST)
- Edit: documents/$objectid (PUT)
- Delete: documents/$objectid (DELETE)
- Thumbnail-Path: documents/$objectid/thumbnail (GET)
- Payload-Path: documents/$objectid/payload (GET)

### Tags:
- Retreive: tags/$id/documents (GET)
- Retreive: tags/$id (GET)
- Add: tags/new (POST)
- Edit: tags/$id (PUT)
- Delete tags/$id (DELETE)

### Categories:
- Retreive: categories/$id/documents (GET)
- Retreive: categories/$id (GET)
- Add: categories/new (POST)
- Edit: categories/$id (PUT)
- Delete categories/$id (DELETE)
